House On Tetrick Road Suddenly Caves In!
Left Column Title
SHORT
At around 9 am this morning, a walker in the Tetrick neighborhood reported that your house fell in. "It looks like it will take a lot to fix this," says the local contractor Jhon Maden. Mr. Maden based his judgement after seeing how the main support of the roof managed to crush the internal water tank.
